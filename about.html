About this
site

The internet is full of noise. Outrage, hot takes, words that are technically advice but feel like judgment. We wanted to create a little place of peace — and there's no one better to help with that than the kind, gentle soul of Keanu Reeves.

"I do not want to be part of a world where being kind is a weakness."

What Would Keanu Do is a small experiment. You type something that's been weighing on you (a question, a frustration, a situation you can't quite shake). And you get back something grounded. Not a listicle or a lecture. Just a few sentences that feel like they came from someone who actually paused before speaking.

We're not claiming Keanu endorses this, runs this, or has ever heard of it. He almost certainly hasn't. But if you've ever watched an interview with him, you know there's a quality there. A stillness. A way of treating whoever he's talking to like they matter.

We tried to bottle that.

Powered by Claude (Anthropic's AI) and inspired by a man who once said "I'm sorry my existence is not very noble or sublime." He was wrong, but that's very him — his humility seems to know no bounds.

Type something and see what comes back.
